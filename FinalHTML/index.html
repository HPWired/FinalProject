Teen Suicide

Suicide is the Second Leading Cause of Death among Teens in the US.

People feel the need to kill themselves when the feel alone in the world or unwanted. This is especially true with teens since they are in a more impressionable and emotional stage. This has an affect on them where they act on impulse. Some causes for suicide attempts in adolescent are feelings of anger, sadness, confusion, problems with attention and hyperactivity among many other things.

Some ways to help someone through this is by:

  • Encourage the Person up
  • Sound reassuring/Positive tone
  • Recognize the person's Feeling/Existence
  • Don't Judge or Yell
  • Listen to the person and stay calm
  • Keep Harmful items away from the person

So how will you know? You can look for these signs:

  • Sleeping and eating habit changes
  • Frequent/Pervasive Sadness
  • Withdrawal from Friends, Families and Activities
  • Complaints of Headache or Fatique
  • Decline in Academic Performance
  • Engrossed in Concept of Death/Dying
  • "I wish I was Dead" or "I won't be a problem for you much longer"

If You are Suffering from Suicidal Tendencies these are some safer methods in easing your Pain:

  • Contact the Suicide Hotline
  • Talk to a Someone
  • Therapy
  • Setup a Schedule
  • Exercise
  • Engage in Hobbies
  • Happy Thoughts
